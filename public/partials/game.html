Problem: There are three boxes of fruit. One box is full of Oranges only. A second box is full of Apples only. A third box is a random mix of both. But all three boxes are mislabelled. For example, the box labelled "Apples" may very well be only oranges, or have both oranges and apples.

Objective: Determine the correct contents of all three buckets by revealing one fruit only from one bucket only. If you choose correctly you will be able to determine the correct contents of all three buckets.

How-To-Play: Start by clicking on a bucket image. Remove the mouse from over the bucket and then hover over the bucket that you selected. A hint will appear and tell you what fruit is actually in the bucket. You only get one hint, so choose wisely. Next hover over any of the 3 fruits above and click and hold that fruit. You can then drop the fruit in to any of the 3 buckets. Once all 3 buckets have been filled, enter the email address associated with your account and press the submit button.
